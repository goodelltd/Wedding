Our Story

Vicki's point of view:

The story of Tyler and Vicki finding each other is as timeless as Romeo and Juliet or Ben and Jerry. Tyler was living life in the fast lane Midland, TX style as a geophysicist for Crownquest, and Vicki was spending her days as a nurse taking care of St. George’s finest and sickest residents at the local hospital. Their stars would align Labor Day Weekend when Tyler had flown into town to visit Vicki’s cousins, Kim and Tom Lund.
Kim and Tom knew that both Vicki and Tyler were single, attractive, and their height to weight ratio seemed complimentary, so they immediately schemed to get them together for a game night.
Vicki mistook Tyler’s rugged good looks for those of an astronaut, and Tyler was mesmerized by Vicki’s spray tan and flowing blonde hair (blondes are the big leagues), so without further ado, the two started a long-distance relationship that blossomed into the website you are visiting today. They are so excited and grateful to be together at last!

Tyler's point of view:

I was just visiting my close friends, Kim and TJ, when Kim brought up that she had a cousin who was single. She then showed me a picture of her and I said, "She's pretty hot and, well, Midland isn't too far away... Plus blondes are the big leagues." Then, fearing a long-distance relationship, I had second thoughts.
The night before I was about to leave St. George, I spent the night playing cards with Vicki and her family, and she didn't mind that I was cheating. I thought, "This girl is pretty chill. I would totally date her if she didn't live a billion miles away." I then left town not thinking too much more about it. After a lot of effort by Kim and TJ to bring us together, Vicki emailed me. This eventually turned into text messages, which eventually became phone calls, and then video calls. After a few months of digital communication, we finally met up again on Thanksgiving and immediately hit if off. That Thanksgiving weekend is when I figured out that I was going to marry Vicki. And so here we are.
